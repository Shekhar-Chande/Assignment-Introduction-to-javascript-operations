Q1. Explain the role of operators in JavaScript. Why are they essential in programming?

Ans:- JavaScript operators are special symbols used in scripts to perform operations on operands, such as arithmetic calculations, logical comparisons, or value assignments. It plays a crucial role in controlling the flow and processing of data within the language. 

JavaScript offers various operators to perform various operations. JavaScript operators can be categorized into 5 types: Arithmetic, Assignment, Comparison, Logical and Conditional.
